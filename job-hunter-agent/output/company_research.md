- Team and role

  - How do you measure backend performance and reliability for high-availability services?
- Product and tech context
  - Which components of the Cloud Intelligence platform will this role most directly impact?
  - Can you describe the typical data flow from cloud usage data ingestion to reporting/analysis?
  - What is the current tech stack beyond Go (e.g., databases, messaging, orchestration, monitoring)?
- Architecture and process
  - How do you approach API versioning, feature flags, and backward compatibility in a distributed system?
  - What is the roadmap for microservices refactoring or new service introductions?
  - How do you handle multi-tenant isolation and security in backend services?
- DevOps and quality
  - What does your CI/CD pipeline look like, and how mature are your testing and deployment practices?
  - How do you implement observability (metrics, tracing, logging) and incident response?
  - What tooling do you use for performance profiling and capacity planning?
- Cloud and cost
  - How do you optimize multi-cloud costs for customers while maintaining performance and reliability?
  - Are there internal initiatives to improve FinOps tooling or automation for internal workloads?
- Growth and culture
  - What opportunities exist for mentorship, leadership, and career growth within the backend track?
  - How does the company support ongoing learning and certifications, given the remote-first culture?
  - Can you share examples of how the company’s values are demonstrated in day-to-day work?
- Logistics and expectations
  - What is the typical on-call burden for backend services, if any?
  - How does visa sponsorship process work for this role, and are there preferred locations within Europe?
  - What does success look like for the first 90 days in this role?
